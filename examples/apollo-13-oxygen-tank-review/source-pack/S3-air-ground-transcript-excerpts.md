# S3: Apollo 13 Technical Air-To-Ground Voice Transcription

Source URL: https://ntrs.nasa.gov/citations/20160014370

## Claim Ledger

- NASA Technical Reports Server classifies the transcript as public and notes public use is permitted.
- The transcript is the direct air-to-ground voice record for Apollo 13.
- Around the accident window, the transcript records crew/ground symptom reporting rather than later root-cause analysis.
- The transcript helps separate what operators knew in the moment from what the review board later concluded.

## Limits

- This excerpt does not reproduce the full 765-page transcript.
- It supports symptom/timing reconciliation only when paired with mission-report and review-board evidence.
