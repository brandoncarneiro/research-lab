# S2: Apollo 13 Mission Report

Source URL: https://ntrs.nasa.gov/archive/nasa/casi.ntrs.nasa.gov/19710003598.pdf

## Claim Ledger

- Apollo 13 was planned as a lunar landing mission in the Fra Mauro area.
- The mission was aborted after abrupt loss of service module cryogenic oxygen associated with a fire in one oxygen tank at about 56 hours into flight.
- Mission reporting ties the oxygen loss to reduced command/service module electrical power, oxygen, and water capability.
- The lunar module became central to the survival/recovery configuration after the service module failure.
- The crew returned to Earth with the crew alive on April 17, 1970.
