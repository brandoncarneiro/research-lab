# S1: Report of Apollo 13 Review Board

Source URL: https://www.nasa.gov/wp-content/uploads/static/history/afj/ap13fj/pdf/report-of-a13-review-board-19700615-19700076776.pdf

## Claim Ledger

- The review board investigated the Apollo 13 accident after the mission failure.
- NASA's historical summary of the board report frames the accident as an unusual combination of mistakes plus a deficient and unforgiving design, not as a random isolated malfunction.
- The board focused the accident chain on service module oxygen tank 2.
- The source record distinguishes the crew-observed in-flight problem from preflight handling, test, voltage, heater, insulation, and design issues.
- The board's recommendations led to oxygen-system redesign and recertification before later Apollo missions.

## Limits

- This excerpt does not reproduce the full report.
- It supports root-cause framing, not a full risk-culture history.
