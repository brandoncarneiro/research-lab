# S4: Apollo 13 Press Kit

Source URL: https://www.nasa.gov/wp-content/uploads/static/history/afj/ap13fj/pdf/a13-press-kit.pdf

## Claim Ledger

- NASA issued the Apollo 13 press kit before the mission accident.
- The press kit describes the mission as a lunar-landing mission and provides planned flight-profile and mission-event material.
- The press kit is useful as planned-objective evidence because it predates the oxygen-tank failure.
- It does not describe the accident outcome because it was a pre-mission document.

## Limits

- This source is stronger for planned intent than for actual outcome.
- It must be paired with post-accident sources for any planned-vs-actual comparison.
