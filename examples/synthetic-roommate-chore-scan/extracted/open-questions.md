# Extracted Open Questions

> Synthetic example only. These questions show how a parent synthesis should preserve unresolved work.

## Product Validation

- Would real roommate households complete setup without one person becoming the household administrator?
- Do households prefer equal rotation, claimed chores, or recurring ownership by person?
- Which reminder tone feels useful rather than patronizing?
- What happens after the first two weeks, when manual systems often decay in the synthetic fixture?

## Evidence Validation

- Do real public reviews, interviews, or forum discussions show the same fairness and reminder patterns?
- Are generic task apps good enough for motivated households?
- Is chore coordination a standalone problem or one part of a broader shared-household workflow?

## Decision Boundaries

- What evidence would justify expanding beyond chores?
- What evidence would justify dropping the idea?
- What data, if any, should a prototype avoid collecting?
