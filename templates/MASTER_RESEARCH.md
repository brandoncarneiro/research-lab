# Legacy Optional Master Research

`MASTER_RESEARCH.md` is a legacy optional artifact. Do not produce it by default, do not treat it as the primary synthesis, and do not use it as a substitute for the required three final artifacts:

- `output/CEO_BRIEF.md`
- `output/RAW_DATA_DIGEST.md`
- `output/CHATGPT_PROJECT_DOC.md`

Only create `output/MASTER_RESEARCH.md` if the project owner explicitly requests an additional long-form report after the three required artifacts already exist.

If explicitly requested, the legacy report must:

- cite Source IDs, Quantitative Signal IDs, Pattern IDs, Contradiction IDs, and Negative Evidence IDs from `RAW_DATA_DIGEST.md`
- separate `FACT`, `INFERENCE`, and `SPECULATION`
- preserve contradictions and negative evidence
- avoid duplicate executive summary content already present in `CEO_BRIEF.md`
- avoid copying raw evidence already present in `RAW_DATA_DIGEST.md`
- stay proportional to evidence confidence
